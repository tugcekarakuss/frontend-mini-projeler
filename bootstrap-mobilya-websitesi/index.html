Our Commitment to Design

Tolv collaborates with some of the most relevant designers of today. Together, we explore new behaviours and patterns of urban lifestyles to find solutions for simple and stylish living.

Tolv upholds the Scandinavian ideals of well-made, well-designed furniture, yet has an appreciation of diverse cultures and an international outlook. For our global customers, we call on designers from across the world to create original furniture based on their expert sensibilities.

Case Study

The Poise chair by Patryk Koca - from conception to completion

Our factory is set up to engage with designs that involve a high degree of manual skills and time-consuming procedures, placing us at the intersection of craftsmanship and mass production.

“The term ‘good design’ probably means different things to different people.”

We believe that a good design achieves the most with the least. It feels resolved and natural—free from gimmicks, forced details, or unnecessary flourishes. It does what it’s meant to do, with logic and ease.

Often, it looks effortless—though that usually takes a lot of effort. The proportions sit right, the materials make sense, and nothing feels overworked or trying too hard to be different.

We don’t follow a checklist. In the end, we choose what feels right to us. But when we look at the designs we’ve selected over time, these are the qualities they tend to share.

Sustainability

We take a world-view on environmental issues and believe in sourcing and treating the raw materials we use with respect and care. Tolv is committed to producing responsibly and fairly, for everything we make and everyone that works for us.

Our factory is set up to engage with designs that involve a high degree of manual skills and time-consuming procedures, placing us at the intersection of craftsmanship and mass production.

Our tables, chairs and cabinets are crafted from solid American oak and solid American walnut. All of our solid oak is sourced from FSC-certified sustainable suppliers.
